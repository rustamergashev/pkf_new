Professional Experience

Kelvin specializes in immigration, wealth management, and family office and so forth for high-net-worth clients. With extensive experience, he is an expert in global business immigration, cross-border investment, and capital market.
Ning Ma earned his J.D. from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, EMBA from the University of Southern California, Master of sociology from the University of Texas, and bachelor of laws from Nanjing University. He previously worked at Boss & Young and Baker & McKenzie, and now serves as a managing director and partner at PKF Demei. He has offered legal services for Jingdong Finance, Lufax, Danone, Haitong Bank, CreditEase, Takashimaya, Philips and other well-known companies. Ning Ma has provided professional solutions for more than 2000 high-net-worth individuals, and spoken more than 700 major summits and conferences with thousands of audiences.
