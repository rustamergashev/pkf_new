PKF Global welcomes PKF Brenson Lawlor in Ireland

 

Our UK and Ireland presence continues to grow with Dublin-based Brenson Lawlor joining the network this week.

Brenson Lawlor, now rebranded as PKF Brenson Lawlor, has supported Irish and international clients for over 50 years. With 115 partners and staff across two offices in Dublin, the firm provides medium and large businesses with an expansive suite of services, including audit, assurance, corporate finance, taxation and insolvency. The team has extensive expertise in a range of industries and is one of the leading financial advisors in the healthcare sector.

Like us, PKF Brenson Lawlor puts people at the heart of its business. The firm is committed to building long-lasting relationships with its staff team and clients alike. Through a culture of working together, sharing insights and learning from each other, the PKF Brenson Lawlor team delivers excellence and quality to its clients.

Ian Lawlor, Managing Partner at PKF Brenson Lawlor, said, ‘We are delighted to join PKF and become a member of this leading global network. By attending the PKF EMEI Gathering in Nice last month, we experienced first-hand the strength and global reach of the brand and the high quality of the firms behind PKF. We are truly excited to be working with such a progressive, energetic and innovative team of people. In partnership with PKF and our new colleagues, we’re really looking forward to driving our business to the next level both nationally and internationally over the coming years.’

PKF Global CEO Theo Vermaak said, ‘From the very first conversation, I knew that PKF Brenson Lawlor was a good fit for PKF Global. Our ambitions, strategies and values are very much aligned. They have expertise in all the core service areas we were seeking in Ireland. Most importantly, the firm’s investment in culture and their people truly resonated. PKF Brenson Lawlor is a great addition to the growing PKF family.’
