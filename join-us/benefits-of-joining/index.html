Benefits of Joining

Our members enjoy a vast array of free tools and resources, as well as the opportunity to share best practices with hundreds of firms within our network. Here are just some of our benefits.

Brand

Work and market under the PKF brand, one of the oldest and most respected brands in the professional services sector.

Build Relationships

Build cross-border business relationships and win international work

Enable referrals

Representation on all internal and external resources, enabling member firms around the network to contact you to refer work, pitch, and tender for joint international projects.

Compliance

Assistance in achieving compliance with our quality requirements. Participation in the Global Monitoring Programme.

Network resources

Access our intranet and use of all publications, technical material, summarised network expertise, country-led Doing Business in Guides, and our flagship Worldwide Tax Guide.

Training, Learning, and Development

Access to our web-based training and L&D platform which hosts CPE-accredited online training and webinars, PKF Bean, IFRS Fundamentals, and MindTools.

Preferential rates with providers

Access to preferential rates negotiated with professional software and educational resource providers.

Events

Attend our virtual and face-to-face events that take place on a regional and international level.
