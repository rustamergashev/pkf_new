PKF Limitada is a member firm of the PKF International Limited (PKFI) network of legally independent firms. PKFI has around 300 member firms and correspondents in 440 locations in around 125 countries providing assurance, accounting and business advisory services.

PKF has been present in Cape Verde since 2010. PKF Cape Verde, PKF Portugal, PKF Angola and PKF Mozambique are in the same network, in which resources, knowledge, expertise and internal forms of organization are shared.
