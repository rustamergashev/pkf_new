PKF Constantia Valley

South Africa - Cape Town
Home PKF Firms Africa South Africa PKF Constantia Valley Cape Town

Providing high quality services in all aspects of financial and business management

PKF Constantia Valley was formed in 2001 by Ken Andersen and Kobus Nell and traded under the name Alliott Andersen Nell Inc. From a small start of seven staff we grew to a current staff compliment of 57. We are currently the largest audit firm in the Southern Suburbs of Cape Town.

Our 5 directors have a combined experience of over 60 years in the South African business environment. Management is ably supported by competent and dedicated staff who share our mission.

PKF Constantia Valley will continually strive to provide a professional, high quality, cost effective, personal service in all aspects of financial and business management. Our service comprise a very entrepreneurial attitude towards most tasks and that gives us a competitive advantage.
