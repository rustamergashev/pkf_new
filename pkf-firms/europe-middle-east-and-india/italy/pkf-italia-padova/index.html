The success of our customers is our success

PKF ITALIA S.p.A. is managed by a Board of Directors composed by Umberto Giacometti (Chairman), Michele Riva, Carlo de Giuseppe and Gianluca Gatti and operates with about 50 professionals coordinated by operative partners.


The whole national territory is covered by the presence of offices located in Milano, Verona, Bologna,Brescia, Padova, Rome and Lecce.

The main features of the services rendered by PKF Italia consist in:

  • Assigning work to the most qualified and suitable personnel for each circumstance
  • Assuring our entire support at any time through close relations with the management and the clients’ internal structure
  • Carrying out our assignment in strict confidence, analyzing and discussing our comments with the management and board to arrive at the best solutions.
