PKF BBA, Chartered Accountants complex problems | simple solutions

We take things personally, especially when it comes to personal tax planning. PKF BBA provides a full range of assurance, tax and advisory services to both individuals and companies across a range of sectors. Our friendly and experienced team looks after the needs of a growing portfolio of small and medium sized businesses, as well as their owners. We can also work with International clients through our Trust Company contacts as to how Jersey based Trust and Company Administration Services can be utilized.

The firm was established in 1989 and has expanded to a team of 36 staff. All of our staff join fully qualified which enables us to immediately assist our client base at a high level.

The business and financial landscape is becoming ever more complex. Contact us and find out how we can become a valued and trusted part of your business team.
