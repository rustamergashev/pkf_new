Home PKF Firms Europe Middle East and India Luxembourg PKF Audit & Conseil - Luxembourg

Providing unsurpassed service which adds premium value to our clients

PKF Audit & Conseil is an audit firm regulated by the Luxembourg financial regulator, the Commission de Surveillance du Secteur Financier (CSSF) and the Luxembourg Institut des Réviseurs d’Entreprises (IRE). We provide audit, accounting, tax, legal, payroll, wealth and corporate finance services.

At PKF we like to keep things clear and concise and our people all share the same values. We combine decades of experience with straightforward, no-nonsense communication. The association of our risk based assurance methodology with our direct communication style ensures that you and your stakeholders will get results you can understand and trust.

We are passionate about assisting our clients with their worldwide service needs and offer a complete, efficient ‘one stop shop’ service. The PKF way is to connect your decision makers to ours, getting the right people in the right seats. Our commitment is to provide you with direct partner and director input while building lasting relationships with you and your company. We believe that only a human approach pays off in the long run. This philosophy is at the heart of our company – and we wouldn’t have it any other way. Our daily work is guided by four fundamental values: honesty, respect, transparency and knowledge sharing. These values, shared by all our staff, create a climate of integrity that facilitates our work and helps ensure that you achieve your goals.
