PKF Fiduciaire SA offers audit, accounting, tax, payroll administration and advisory services in Switzerland.

The size of our company allows us to offer diversified services with all the necessary skills, as well as ensuring our availability to maintain strong personal contact with each client. Our goal is to bring you the best possible service while maintaining quality human relationships.

We have offices in Geneva, Lausanne, Vevey, Fribourg, Sion, Neuchâtel and Moutier.
