PKF Australia ranked in top 10 accounting firms in Australia

We are delighted to announce that PKF Australia has been recognised among the top 10 accounting and advisory firms in Australia by The Australian Financial Review (AFR).

This marks an incredibly successful year for PKF Australia, with the firm achieving year-on-year revenue growth of 12% and total revenue for FY22 standing at an impressive $141m. These fantastic results mean that the firm has climbed up the AFR rankings and has broken into the top 10 once again.  

The firm’s clients and staff alike can look forward to an exciting year ahead as PKF Australia strives to enhance its service offering. The coming year will see PKF Australia delivering an expanded range of advisory services, including succession planning, corporate finance and risk management. Team members will have access to even more learning and development opportunities, and can participate in a host of environment, social and governance programmes seeking to benefit the wider community. 

Steve Meyn, PKF Australia Chairman, said, “We hope this recognition fills our clients, our staff and our community with confidence and assurance, knowing that PKF Australia is committed to delivering the exceptional experiences and innovations that will propel their success into the future. … We are always learning and evolving to make a meaningful impact for all in our community.” 

PKF International CEO, Theo Vermaak, said, “Congratulations to everyone at PKF Australia. While size alone is not the most important goal, critical mass is required to ensure the resources to continue innovating and improving. This combined with your outstanding workplace culture and unwavering focus on your people will allow you to propel your future and that of your clients and communities.” 

PKF Australia’s team of over 90 partners and 750 staff members provides business advisory, accounting and assurance services to a diverse range of clients. To read more about how PKF Australia can support your business, please click here.
