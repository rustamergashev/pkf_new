PKF Global empowers members through global partnerships

We are delighted to welcome G-P, Beeye and SAPRO to our growing suite of global partners.

Collaborating with our new partners will open exciting opportunities for our member firms and further support them to deliver world-class services to their clients.

As the pioneer and recognised leader in the global employment industry, G-P helps companies build and manage teams anywhere in the world. G-P’s comprehensive solutions alleviate the complexities associated with global recruitment, payroll management and onboarding procedures, by providing a one-stop shop service covering legal, tax and HR issues.

Nick Adams, Vice President of Partnerships at G-P, said, ‘This transformative collaboration with PKF Global presents a remarkable avenue for forward-thinking member firms to empower their clients in venturing into novel and dynamic markets, offering essential services and unwavering assistance. Our mission revolves around bolstering PKF member firms, enabling their burgeoning ecosystem of inventive and pioneering enterprises to successfully realise their ambitions of global expansion.’

Our partnership with web-based resource scheduling provider Beeye will revolutionise our approach to global resourcing. Using Beeye’s innovative technology, our members will be empowered to deliver the best possible service and value to their clients. Partnering at a global level will help us realise our ambition of bringing international reach and capabilities to our member firms around the world.

David Kerr, Chief Customer Officer at Beeye, said, ‘We are thrilled to announce this partnership with PKF Global. Resourcing and scheduling continue to be a key priority for PKF Global members across the world. This multi-year commitment means that we can work closely with PKF Global to jointly solve these priorities using the power of AI to give member firms more time back and increase client satisfaction.’

SAPRO is a renowned global talent outsourcing firm, providing expert tax, audit and advisory professionals. Our partnership with SAPRO will give our members access to an extensive pool of highly skilled professionals, to be called on as required based on business needs. By leveraging SAPRO’s expertise in talent outsourcing, we will equip our member firms with the resources they need to realise their full potential, elevate their services and achieve their ambitions.

Julie Bonness, Senior Vice President of Strategic Partnerships at SAPRO, said, ‘By aligning our capabilities with PKF Global's extensive reach and shared values, we are poised to make a lasting impact within the industry. Together, we will enable member firms to overcome talent challenges, optimise service delivery and ultimately propel their success on a global scale.’

Theo Vermaak, PKF Global CEO, said, ‘By partnering with the brightest minds and embracing the latest technology, we stay ahead of the curve, and the competition, and help our members deliver the best service offering, and resources, to their clients. We look forward to working closely with all our global partners to drive further success for our member firms.’
