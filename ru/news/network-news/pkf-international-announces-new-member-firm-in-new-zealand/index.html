PKF International announces new member firm in New Zealand

Our presence in New Zealand continues to grow as we welcome PKF Kendons as a new member of the network. 

Formerly a PKF correspondent firm, PKF Kendons delivers services in four key areas: business development, technology & software, tax & compliance and audit & reviews. The Wellington-based firm includes two partners who, together with a team of talented, professional and highly qualified individuals, strive to achieve success for their clients. 

This new addition to the PKF family represents a strong strategic fit, with PKF Kendons’ values of being empowering, forward thinking, passionate, family focused and ‘straight up’ closely aligned with those of PKF. 

Michael Markham, Chief Executive of PKF Kendons, commented, “Our firm has been an institution in the Wellington region for over 90 years. As technology evolves, together with client growth and expansion, national and international markets become even more important for us. The need to have progressive, like-minded firms to connect and share best practice with is paramount. We are eager to continue to connect with fellow PKF member firms here in New Zealand and globally as we continue to serve our clients on their journey.”   

PKF International CEO, Theo Vermaak, said, “We are really proud to welcome PKF Kendons to the PKF International network as full members. We look forward to working closely with Michael and the PKF Kendons team, and our existing member firms in New Zealand, to drive future success.”
