PKF member firms upheld as exemplary workplaces

 

We are proud to announce that several of our member firms have recently been recognised as outstanding places to work.
PKF-CAP LLP, our member firm in Singapore, has been officially certified a Great Place To Work. This accreditation reflects the firm’s continuous efforts to create a nurturing and empowering work environment, where every individual can thrive and be their best. This latest award follows PKF-CAP being named one of Asia’s Top Employers by leading brand recognition think tank, Influential Brands.

Our North American member firm PKF O’Connor Davies is proud to have been recognised once again as one of the Best Places to Work in New Jersey by NJBIZ, New Jersey’s leading business journal. The firm has also been named one of the Best Places to Work in New York City by Crain’s New York Business. These accolades are a testament to the firm’s exceptional culture, benefits and work environment.

Dawn V. Perri, partner and Chief Human Resources Officer at PKF O’Connor Davies, said, ‘It’s so rewarding to be continually recognised as a leading workplace and lauded for our approach to the employee experience. The unique combination of training, mentoring and professional development we provide, coupled with our extensive compensation and benefits package, continues to attract top-notch talent and solidify our standing as a nationally recognised organisation.’

PKF San Diego has been honoured as one of the Best Places to Work 2023 by the San Diego Business Journal. The awards programme celebrates outstanding companies whose benefits, policies and practices are among the best in the region.
PKF Kerikeri, one of our member firms based in New Zealand, has been awarded the Balance Award at The Gap’s annual Reunion conference, an event which brings together all the key players in New Zealand’s accounting industry. The team’s success demonstrates the firm’s true flexibility and commitment to balance in the workplace.

PKF Francis Clark, our UK member firm serving clients in the south-west of England, has enjoyed a highly successful year, racking up countless top employer awards. Its latest achievement is being named one of the UK’s Best Workplaces in Consulting & Professional Services 2023. This award recognises the firm’s focus on wellbeing, work-life balance, professional development, job satisfaction and creating a supportive culture. Based on employee feedback, PKF Francis Clark achieved an overall trust index score of 82%, far surpassing the 54% rating of a typical UK company.

Comments from PKF Francis Clark staff collated as part of the anonymous survey included, ‘All staff, including senior management, are kind and welcoming, regardless of your level in the organisation. I feel managers are genuinely interested in helping me to perform at my best professionally and that they genuinely care about me as a person.’
