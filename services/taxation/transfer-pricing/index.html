Compliance Services

Transfer Pricing Documentation
Prepare and maintain local and master file documentation in line with OECD guidelines and local regulations.

Country-by-Country Reporting (CbCR)
Assistance with the preparation and submission of CbCR to meet global reporting requirements.

Risk Assessment
Evaluate your current transfer pricing policies to identify potential risks and suggest improvements.

Advisory Services

Strategic Planning
Develop transfer pricing strategies that align with your business goals and enhance tax efficiency.

Transfer Pricing Policy Development
Design and implement transfer pricing policies that are robust, compliant, and optimize tax liabilities.

Benchmarking Analysis
Conduct thorough benchmarking studies to determine arm's length pricing for your intercompany transactions.

Tax Efficient Supply Chain Management (TESCM)
Optimize your supply chain structures to align with business operations while maximizing tax efficiency. We provide in-depth analysis and restructuring strategies to enhance operational effectiveness and tax optimization in your supply chain.

Controversy

TP Audit
comprehensive support to navigate the complexities of TP audits with confidence.

Advanced Pricing Agreements (APAs)
Secure certainty in your cross-border transactions through upfront agreements with tax authorities, reducing future tax disputes and providing peace of mind.

Mutual Agreement Procedures (MAPs)
Resolve international tax disputes efficiently through treaty-based negotiation procedures, avoiding the pitfalls of double taxation and fostering a more stable business environment.

TP Forensic
In the context of litigation, TP forensic involves detailed analysis and investigation into the transfer pricing policies and practices of a company

TP Litigation
With in-depth tax law knowledge and courtroom experience, we’re here to protect your interests every step of the way.
